ORANGE PALLET INC ORANGE LOGISTICS (PTY) LTD.

Orange's shareholders are well respected Logistics Entrepreneurs with over Thirty years experience in the formation of companies that have innovated and created substantial supply chain value for dozens of Blue Chip companies over the years.

PRESTO PARCELS: FOUNDED 1978

Became South Africa's largest parcel carrier/integrator. Clients included, Cosmetics: Coty, Max-Factor Revlon, Shulton (Old Spice) Yardley. Motor Vehicle Parts Accessories, Caterpillar, Cummins, Ford, General Motors, Komatsu, Nissan, Mercedes Benz, Toyota, and Volkswagen.

The Business was sold to London Listed Company: Mitchell Cotts Limited in 1982.

PRESTO TRANSPORT HOLDINGS LTD 1983

Inter City Container based trucking company. Became South Africa's largest Multi City Daily service carrier. Clients included:

  • Motor Manufacturers: Original Equipment Component Delivery: Ford, General Motors, Mercedes Benz, Toyota
  • Tyres: Dunlop, Bridgestone Firestone, Tiger Wheels
  • Fast Moving Consumer Goods: Chet Industries, Colgate Palmolive, Colman Foods (Tiger Brands), Energade, Langeberg (Tiger Brands), Gilbeys (Diageo) South African Breweries.
  • Consumer Durables: AECI Paint (Dulux), Eveready

1986 Introduced: “OWNER DRIVER” Concept to South African Breweries to the late CEO Graham Mackay: Awarded Extensive SAB Contracts.

PRESTO LISTED ON THE JSE: OCTOBER 1987

Anchor Shareholders: MANAGEMENT: TRENCOR LTD FINANSBANK (NEDBANK)

1990 PRESTO TRANPORT HOLDINGS LTD liquidated. Sustained industrial action across the South African landscape rendered a Inter City "JUST IN TIME" Carrier unworkable.

1992 CARGO AFRICA (PTY) LTD

Joined former PRESTO executive and built over fourteen years South Africa's largest "MANAGED LOGISTICS COMPANY"

Sold 60% interest to the late Bill Lynch of Imperial Holdings.

Diversified Blue Chip 100% contractor client base.

Client Base

  • "FMCG": Brandhouse, Colgate Palmolive, Tiger Brands (Tastic), Diageo, Heineken, Windhoek, Colman Foods, Milling Logistics (Pty) Ltd. UNILEVER (DHL)

  • Industrial: Consumer Durables: Bridgestone Firestone, Dunlop, Plascon, JD Group, Italtile, PG Glass, Shaterpruffe- South African Post Office

Created South Africa's largest OWNER DRIVER Company "CADE". Founded as a BEE structure and sold to a Investec BEE Funded Entity in 2008.
